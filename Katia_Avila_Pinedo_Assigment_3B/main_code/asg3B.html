Welcome to FroppyLand!

You are a FroppyLand character named: Squanchy!

  • Click and drag with your mouse to move the camera
  • Or use Q to pan Left and R to pan Right
  • Use W,S,A, and D to move foward, backward, left, and right respectively.
  • Press RESET GAME button to reset the POV *WARNING* THIS ACTION WILL RESET GAMEPLAY

    • FROOPYLAND MAZE:

  • Follow the Froppy character on the purple blocks to find your way around the maze.
  • Start is at the golden tower + Froppy Character tower, located at spawnpoint.
  • Finish is at the golden Froppy wall, located across the map from the spawnpoint.

    • Time your maze run!

  • The timer begins on the first keypress of W, A, S, or D.
  • Timer will not begin if Q or E are pressed.
  • When you reach the finishline, press "END TIMER" button to see your maze run time.
